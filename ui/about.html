our vision

The vision for Code Queen is to increase capacity to be able to serve 1,000 women a year by 2025.

about us

Educating The Children is a UK registered charity that focuses on empowering young women through education and training that increases their employability. Last year we launched Code Queen in Kampala which is an all-female bootcamp designed to train young women in coding and soft skills. Code Queen has successfully run 4 cohorts and graduated 83 students since the launch in 2019.

Due to the COVID 19, we were forced to move our coding bootcamp online last year in April 2020. To do this we rely on facilitators to deliver the online classes, the classes recorded via zoom and then shared with the students to review and replay as they practice their tasks. We want you to automate this process into a scalable solution
